About Me

Academic Profile

Dedicated to excellence in research, higher education, and innovation in Automatic Control, Intelligent Systems, Renewable Energy, and Power Electronics.

Professor of Automatic Control and Intelligent Systems

Prof. Kamel Guesmi is a Full Professor whose research focuses on nonlinear control systems, intelligent optimization, renewable energy systems, artificial intelligence, and advanced power electronics.

Throughout his academic career, he has contributed to numerous scientific publications, supervised graduate students, participated in national and international research projects, and served the academic community through teaching, research leadership, and scientific collaboration.
